DEA-AI Docs

Getting Started

Welcome to DEA-AI, your decentralized AI assistant for Ethereum transactions.

Checking Balance

To check your own balance, type:

balance

To check another wallet's balance, type:

Check balance of [wallet address]

Sending ETH

To send ETH, type:

Send [amount] ETH to [wallet address]

Scheduling Transfer

To schedule a transfer, type:

Schedule transfer of [amount] ETH to [wallet address] on [date/time]
